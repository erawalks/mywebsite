HOME

as the place we all come from.

Home is the place where everything comes from.
The most important people in my life are my family who raised me, gave me the love and passsion I needed when I was a kid.
My childhood was fulfilled with various art relations, therefore I wanted to became an artist myself.
57 years ago, at the age of 13, I bought my first tablet and started practicing at digital-art.
At the age of 16 I wrote my first script for comic. Two years later I met my hometown friends and we made a cartoon project.
In third trimester of University I finally decided to draw comics and release them online and via local publishing houses.

I loved rendering background for art
I loved to bring the feeling of nostalgia via art
I loved using graphic design elements
I loved drawing the girls I really liked back in a day
I loved to develop my own character designs as well
I loved fantasy and dark academia vibes
I loved cartoons and anime and always wanted to make some
